FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from Abigail.

Abigail sells nine market staples: Gari, Beans, Rice, Millet, Maize, Groundnuts, Soya Beans, Wheat, and Agushie. All products are available in retail quantities for households, and in bulk quantities for businesses, caterers, and events.
You can place an order by calling or sending a WhatsApp message to +233 24 140 4275. Tell Abigail what products you want, the quantity, and your delivery address. You can also visit her directly at Mankessim Market.
Yes! Abigail offers door-to-door delivery within Mankessim and to surrounding towns including Saltpond, Cape Coast, Anomabo, and other areas. Contact her to confirm delivery to your specific location.
Absolutely. Abigail specialises in bulk sales and supplies caterers, chop bars, shop owners, schools, and event organisers. All nine products are available in large quantities. Contact her for bulk pricing and arrangements.
Products are sold in small retail quantities (tins, small bags) as well as large bags and bulk sacks. The exact sizes and pricing depend on the product and current market rates. WhatsApp Abigail for the latest prices and availability.
Payment can be made in cash on delivery or at the market. For bulk pre-orders, arrangements can be made via mobile money (MoMo). Contact Abigail directly to confirm payment options for your order.
Abigail is based at Mankessim Market in the Mfantsiman Municipal District, Central Region, Ghana. She also travels to customers for door-to-door sales in and around the area.
